Referrals

Invite friends to FilesAnywhere and get a discount off your monthly enrollment

For every person who joins FilesAnywhere, we will give you 20% off your monthly bill. If five people join, that’s 100% off your individual enrollment! As long as that persons account stays active, the discount will continue to apply to your monthly subscription.

Referral discounts only apply toward primary accounts.
They do not count toward reducing group enrollment costs.
